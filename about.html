About Us

Hi, I'm Sarah Louise

I help ordinary people build extraordinary bodies without sacrificing their lifestyle. Having worked with many different clients with so many different body types, I know what works and what doesn't.

As a personal trainer with over 10 years experience and certificates in nutrition (specifically the plant based diet), mobility, fat burn and overeating, my aim is to take you on a personalised fitness journey to a body you love & a lifestyle you can maintain.

Take your fitness to the next level, regain confidence and create a consistent routine through personalised workouts & healthy habits. All unique to you.

My training philosophy

Every journey is unique

I know it's easy it is to compare ourselves to others, but it's important to remember that there aren't two fitness journeys that are the same. I will meet you where you are in your fitness journey and personalise your workouts, nutrition & weekly goals so you can see progress in the way you need to. It will make working out & eating healthy an enjoyable & realistic part of your life

Realistic & healthy nutrition

I'm a firm believer that you can still maintain a social life, even when you're on a fitness journey. You can still enjoy drinks with your partner or pizza nights with friends. These things will always be a part of your life, so instead I'll teach you how to optimise your nutrition around them, how to include more protein & overall eat all foods in a healthy balance... without compromising your lifestyle. I take great pride in my clients never feeling like they're "on a diet"

Results that last forever

As your trainer, I care about not only saying "do this, do that" but instead "do this because, do that because". I will help you understand why I give you the exercises I do or suggest the meals I do. We will slowly adjust everything from where you are now to where you want to be & I'll explain everything along the way. This means you won't bounce back after and you will maintain your results even when I'm not there.
